We collect the following information when you use the Service:

Personal information is used for the following purposes:

We do not provide personal information to third parties without prior consent, except in the following cases:

個人情報は、以下の場合を除き、事前の同意なく第三者に提供しません：

- When required by law / 法令に基づく場合
